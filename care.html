Care & Policies

Below are care guidelines and policy details to help preserve the beauty and longevity of your jewelry.

Jewelry Care

At J.POETIX, handcrafted jewelry made from silver and natural stones embraces organic design. Marks that appear over time are part of each amulet’s story, capturing artistry, craftsmanship, and individuality.

Avoiding Water Exposure

Avoid wearing jewelry while showering, bathing, surfing, or swimming. Stones such as opals are porous and may change color when wet. If your jewelry gets wet, dry it thoroughly as soon as possible.

Proper Jewelry Storage

Store jewelry in a dry environment or inside a plastic bag to reduce oxidation and tarnishing. Silver may naturally develop a patina over time. If desired, this can be removed with a polishing cloth.

Repairs and Cleaning

J.POETIX offers free repairs on jewelry purchased within 3 months. After that, a small fee may apply depending on the repair. Jewelry cleaning and polishing is available for a $40 fee, not including shipping and handling.

Shipping

Shipping is covered for USA orders totaling $500 or more. Orders are generally shipped through private carriers such as UPS or FedEx. We are unable to ship to P.O. Boxes.

Canadian orders are shipped by First Class Mail International and typically arrive within 1 to 2 weeks. International customers are responsible for any import fees or tariffs required by their country.

As a small studio, each piece is crafted by hand and processed in the order it is received. In-stock items are typically shipped within 3 days, while made-to-order or custom pieces may take 4 to 6 weeks.

Returns & Exchanges

Exchanges for size differences or store credit are available within 14 days of receiving unworn, like-new merchandise. Customers are responsible for shipping expenses, and shipping charges are not reimbursed.

Because J.POETIX is a small slow-fashion jewelry brand with many made-to-order items, refunds are not currently available. Sample or archive sale items, discounted items of 10% or more, fine jewelry, and custom pieces are final sale.

Cancellations

Cancellation requests made after 12 hours will incur a cancellation fee of $20 or 25% of the order total, whichever is lower. Production begins promptly after an order is received.
